About Mr. Kleinhans

Mr. Kleinhans has been teaching physics for 14 years and has inspired countless students to pursue careers in science and technology. He was also a successful computer science startup founder and businessman before dedicating his time to teaching and raising his kids.

Mr. Kleinhans was always eager to share his knowledge and experience with his students, whether through his engaging lectures, his heartwarming "Feel Good Fridays" speeches, or through his leadership of many clubs and activities

Memories of Mr. Kleinhans

Mr. Kleinhans will always be remembered for his passion for teaching, his kindness, and his sense of humor. Whether he was telling jokes during class, bringing donuts as incentives for his students, or sitting us down for a great Feel Good Friday, he always made learning fun and rewarding.
